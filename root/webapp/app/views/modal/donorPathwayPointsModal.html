Donor Pathway Points

Point 1 - Consultation

When you arrive at Point 1 this will mean that you have had your initial consultation with a Living Donor Coordinator at your hospital. During that consultation you will have been provided with further information and then registered on Donor View. This will be the start of your journey through the Donor Pathway.

Point 2 - Testing

When you arrive at Point 2 this will mean that you are entering a period of testing. This testing will cover such things as routine bloods, cross matching with the recipient, x-rays and ECCGs. These tests will determine your suitability as a donor. Your test results will be displayed on the system when available.

Point 3 - Review

When you arrive at Point 3 this will mean that your results are being reviewed by a clinician to assess your suitability as a donor. When that decision has been made it will outline whether you are able to move forward to the next point, whether you need further testing, whether you are on hold for other reasons or if the decision is no to donating. The outcome of the review will also have been conveyed to you outside of Donor View.

Point 4 - Further Testing

When you arrive at Point 4 this is a period of further more detailed testing with this leading into planning for your operation for the donation of your kidney. Any further test results will be displayed on the system when available. When those tests have been completed the system will inform you whether further investigation is needed, whether you can move forward to planning your operation or if the decision is no to donating. The outcome of the review will also have been conveyed to you outside of Donor View.

Point 5 - Planning

When you arrive at Point 5 this is the period of planning for your donor operation. During this point you will experience the following :-

  • Joint meeting between Donor and Recipient Meeting
  • Surgical Review
  • Psychology Assessment
  • Human Tissue and detailed cross matching with recipient
  • Pre â€“ operation consultation

During this point the date for your operation will be agreed.

Point 6 - Operation

When you arrive at Point 6 this is when your operation will take place.

Your date for operation will have been conveyed to you in Point 5 and will be confirmed on Donor View at this point.

Point 7 - Post Operation

When you arrive at Point 7 you will be in the post operation period. During this point there will be a number of reviews to check on progress.

1st Review - within 6 weeks
  • Review with surgeon
  • Blood tests and BPs
  • Post Care information
2nd Review - up to 3 months
3rd Review - up to 6 months
4th Review - up to 12 months
