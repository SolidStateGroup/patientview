Your answers will be sent to (1) PatientView, so you can monitor your symptoms and overall health yourself, if you wish; (2) Your kidney team, so they can get a better idea of how you have been, and what they can do to support you; and (3) The UK Renal Registry, who will use your data for audit and research as described in their privacy statement: https://www.renalreg.org/patient-info/
